Neuroscience 2022

Brain Based dynamic art studio to generate Smart Contracts using emotional analysis

People often struggle expressing emotions and lack of time to spend in learning new techniques to do so. Moreover, modern technologies are difficult to use for the general audience in order to express meaningful ideas. Emerging technologies like AI Art generation, NFTs and Electro Encephalograms (EEG) lack in direction as they are used as the final outcome and not as a tool. We propose a Brain Machine Interface (BMI) Art Studio that transforms the environment with your emotions. So that, people can create art and save it in the blockchain. With the use of brainwaves people can paint in a Virtual Reality environment, transform their painting with Image2Image Generation using a description and the emotion they are feeling. The emotion detection is done with Resnet, a Time Series Classification state of art model that can identify 3 cognitive states (Positive, Neutral and Negative).
